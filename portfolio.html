About Me

How did I start on Web Developement?

As a kid, I used to spend time on the web quite often and at some point I got interested how these sites are made.
I started with ready made web-builders like Weebly and Wix but eventually I switched to learning how to build websites with "pure HTML and CSS".
After two to three years, I began to learn how server side works in Web Developement with PHP and later on made my first project using it with MySQL database.
About three and half years into Web Developement and I created my first ever public project / community which was a "Get-Paid-To" website where users could earn rewards by completing various tasks like surveys and playing games.
However after about year, I had to shut down the site due to lack of time and the amount of potential vulnerabilities the site had.
Nowadays I am learning mostly React/Next.js and deploy small projects on Vercel, potentially launching a new website some day.
